THE 
AMERICAN
FLAG   AND                     GEOMETRY

  


The American Heritage Dictionary defines flag- "as a piece of cloth of distinct size, color and design used as a symbol, standard, signal or emblem."  The purpose of this web site is to examine the familiar symbol of the American Flag through the eyes of the geometer.  From that perspective, this site will demonstrate that the American flag, long viewed as the embodiment of patriotism, also illustrates many geometric concepts including:

  • Collinearity

  • Betweeness

  • Circles

  • Similarity/Proportions

  • Perpendicularity 

  • Congruence

  • Rectangles

  • Parallel Lines 

  • Polygons

  • Plane/Curved Surface 

  • Trigonometry

  • Triangles
