  3. Because the sun is very far away from us, its rays are nearly parallel, so we have similar triangles, so we can write a proportion:
    28:35 = y:25  or 28/35 = y/25  therefore 28 x 25 = 35y, therefore y=20 feet
    so the flag pole is 20 feet high.
  4. tan 30 = height/ (x + 1)
    tan 35 = height/x
    solving these two equations in two unknowns, simultaneously, height is approximately 3.29 meters.  Hence the flagpole is 3.29 meters tall or just a little over 10 feet tall.
