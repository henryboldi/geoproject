  • sine = opposite/hypotenuse
  • cosine = adjacent/hypotenuse
  • tangent = opposite/adjacent

Also recall the definition of:

  • angle of depression-an angle between the downward line of sight and the horizontal.
  • angle of elevation- an angle between the downward line of sight and the horizontal.

TYPICAL PROBLEM:   If there is a 30 degree angle of elevation from a point 50 feet away from a flagpole, how high is the flagpole?

                                    

Solution: tan (30) = flag pole/50    the pole is 28.9 feet high

 

ANOTHER PROBLEM: When a five foot flagpole is mounted in a bracket on a wall, the top of the flag is 3 feet from the top of the wall.  What is the angle formed between the flagpole and the wall?

 

 

 

Solution: sin (x) = 3/5  x is approximately 37 degrees.
