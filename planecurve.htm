Plane versus Curved Surfaces

     Recall the definition of a plane as a surface such that if any two points on the surface are connected by a line, all points of the line are also on the surface.  A plane must, therefore, be flat and have only two dimensions, width and length, or hoist and fly in flag terminology.

                                          

 

 

     The American Flag is sewn on a flat piece of cloth.  Lying flat then, it represents a plane surface, but when it is flown from a flag pole, it is clearly a surface that is not plane--a curved surface.
